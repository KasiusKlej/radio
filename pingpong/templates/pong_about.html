About Ping Pong ✕

Ping Pong

Classic Pong-style game
Ported from original VB6 code

How to Play

  • The goal is to reach 52 points.
  • The ball speeds up over time.
  • First player to reach the target score wins.

Controls

Player 1 W / S
Player 2 ↑ / ↓

Game Modes

  • Human – Two players on one keyboard (hotseat)
  • Computer – Play against AI
  • Network – Reserved for future multiplayer
